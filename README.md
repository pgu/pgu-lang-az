Purpose
---
Game for learning alphabets, step by step. The rule is simply to associate the symbols to their latin counterparts.

Current alphabets
---
- Hiragana あ お い う え...
- Katakana ア オ イ ウ エ...
- Korean ㄱ ㄴ ㄷ ㄹ ㅁ...
- Russian Б Г Д Ж Ф...
- Arabic ﺏ ﺥ ﺱ ﻁ ﻑ ﻙ...
